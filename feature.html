Exlog Tunisia

Exlog Tunisia can provide complete surface production testing services for virtually any type of well testing application, including DST, remote testing / fluid storage and shipping set-ups, early production facility (EPF) and in-line testing programs. We also offer a technically-advanced mobile multi-phase metering package; fully self-contained, trailer-mounted (including truck/crane), and equipped with electronic micro-process control and data acquisition capability. Exlog Tunisia can also provide high-quality electronic data acquisition capability with any of our well testing packages. In addition, we can provide complete deliverability analysis services (oil well inflow performance relationship; gas well absolute open flow potential), including program design recommendations upon client request
